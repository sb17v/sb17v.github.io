Biography

Hi, I am a second year PhD student in the Department of Computer Science at Florida State University and currently working as a Research Assistant in Computer Architecture and SysTems Research Laboratory (CASTL) under Dr. Weikuan Yu.

I have received B.Tech in Information Technology from St.Thomas’ College of Engineering and Technology, Kolkata, India in 2015. After completing the bachelor’s degree program, I have worked as a Java and NodeJS developer at Infosys Limited and have an experience of almost two years in designing and developing the different layers (mostly microservices and application layer) of web applications using latest technologies and trends in software engineering.
